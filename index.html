How-to Play

  • Click squares to try and hit enemy ships
  • If you hit a ship, the square will become green with a number representing the length of the ship
  • If you miss, the square will become red with an 'X'
  • To win, sink all 5 of the enemy ships
  • You have a limited number of clicks, and you gain 2 clicks every time you hit a ship
